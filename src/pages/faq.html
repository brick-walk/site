Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about commissions, services, and more.

How do I commission a custom piece?

Start by reaching out through my contact form with details about your project. Include your timeline, budget range, and any reference images or ideas. I'll review your request and respond within 3-5 business days to discuss next steps.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Turnaround time varies depending on the complexity and scale of the project. Smaller pieces typically take 2-3 weeks, while larger or more complex projects may take 1-2 months. I'll provide a specific timeline estimate after our initial consultation.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! I ship worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated based on the destination and the size/weight of the piece. All pieces are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive safely.

What is your payment structure?

For commissions, I typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion before shipping. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment schedule. I accept bank transfers, PayPal, and credit cards.

Can you recreate a piece from your portfolio?

Each piece I create is unique, and I generally don't create exact replicas. However, I'm happy to create new pieces inspired by previous work or explore similar themes and techniques. Let's discuss what aspects appeal to you.

Do you offer workshops or speaking engagements?

Not currently, due to my collegiate studies. However, I offer private zoom demonstrations, consulting advice, and building tutorials. Visit my Commissions page for more information, or contact me directly to discuss any consultation questions.

How can I stay updated on your work?

Follow me on social media for regular updates, behind-the-scenes content, and announcements about new pieces. You can also check my portfolio for the latest work.

What if I have a question not listed here?

Feel free to reach out! I'm always happy to answer questions. Use the contact form or email me directly, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.
