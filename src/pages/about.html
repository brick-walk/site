Kellen Ulrich

Artist | Designer | Influencer

Welcome to BrickWalk! BrickWalk is a company inspired by individuals from all different walks of life who are passionate about creativity through Lego®.

As a part of BrickWalk, I design custom-built LEGO models, sculptures, and kits. I take immense pride in my work, holding myself to the highest standards of quality, ingenuity, and creativity in all that I do.

My journey began with a simple curiosity and love of LEGO that has evolved into a lifelong passion for using bricks as an artistic medium. With over 10 years of experience creating unique and memorable builds, I continue to aspire to learn new ways to express myself as an artist through LEGO.

Throughout my career, I've had the privilege of working with clients across the U.S. Each project has brought new challenges and opportunities for creative expression. I’ve engaged in commission work since 2026 and look forward to future opportunities to meet new people and showcase building skills to the world.

When I'm not creating, you can find me exploring new techniques, playing classical guitar, or pursuing photography. I believe that creativity knows no bounds, and every project is an opportunity to push the limits of what's possible.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to collaborate on a project!
